Focused Legal Service for Maryland Property Owners

Gregory prepares Maryland deeds and related real estate documents for clients handling family transfers, spouse additions or removals, corrective deeds, estate planning transfers, life estate deeds, and other property-related matters.

He understands that many clients come to him during important life transitions, including marriage, divorce, inheritance, aging, refinancing, or planning for the next generation. His goal is to make the legal side of that process as clear, efficient, and comfortable as possible.

Experience That Matters

With over 30 years experience in real estate and estate planning, Gregory understands both the legal requirements of deed preparation as well as the technical details involved in the recording process, and ensuring that you receive the benefit of any available transfer tax exemptions.

Every matter receives careful review and personal attention, with the goal of preparing documents that are accurate, effective, and appropriate for the client’s specific situation.

Professional, Accessible, and Practical

Gregory believes clients deserve legal services that are professional without being intimidating, detailed without being confusing, and efficient without sacrificing quality.

If you have a Maryland deed, real estate, or estate planning question, Gregory welcomes the opportunity to discuss your situation and help you determine the proper next step.
